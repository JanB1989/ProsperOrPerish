# This leads to absolutely empty provinces without anything grow quite big, without player/ai investment of buildings
# We already have the change that staffed buildings increase pop cap, i think pop cap should lean slightly more on that and less on vegetation
# This also has the benefit of indirectly reducing the amount of available RGO slots, which is good aswell
